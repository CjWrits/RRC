About Royal Rajasthan Collection

Preserving heritage through authentic craftsmanship and timeless designs

Our Heritage Story

Royal Rajasthan Collection was born from a deep passion for preserving the rich textile heritage of Rajasthan. Founded in the heart of Jaipur, we have been dedicated to bringing authentic Rajasthani craftsmanship to fashion enthusiasts worldwide.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to celebrate the timeless artistry of Rajasthani artisans while making their exquisite creations accessible to modern women who appreciate traditional elegance.

Every piece in our collection tells a story of centuries-old techniques passed down through generations of skilled craftsmen. From intricate hand embroidery to vibrant block prints, each garment represents the soul of Rajasthan's cultural legacy.

Authenticity

Every piece is crafted using traditional techniques and authentic materials, ensuring genuine Rajasthani heritage in every stitch.

Artisan Support

We work directly with local artisans, providing fair wages and supporting their families while preserving traditional crafts.

Sustainability

Our commitment to eco-friendly practices includes using natural dyes, organic fabrics, and sustainable production methods.

Quality Excellence

Each garment undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure it meets our high standards of craftsmanship and durability.
